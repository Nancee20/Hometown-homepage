The Eastern Heartland; A place where culture is displayed to its peak

Top three activities to do at Imo State

Visit Oguta Lake

Oguta Lake is one of the largest natural lakes in Nigeria that divides the Oguta town into two,and unlike most natural lakes,its water is clean and fresh and has two colours that can never mix with each other

Go to the National Museum, Owerri

The museum is situated in Orlu and tells the story of the rich history of Igboland through paintings, sculptures, and artefacts, it also houses historical items from before and after independence

See the Imo Palm Plantation

Imo Palm Plantation is located in between Amaofor and Egbema towns, on both sides of the Owerri/Port Harcourt expressway. The plantation was a significant source of foreign exchange before the discovery of crude oil.
